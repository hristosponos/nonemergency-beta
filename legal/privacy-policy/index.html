2. Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

2.1 Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following data:

  • Email Address – Provided when subscribing to Our newsletter.
  • Payment Information – Collected by Stripe, which processes payments on their platform. We receive details such as billing address, email, phone number, and transaction history.
  • Usage Data – Includes IP address, browser type, time spent on pages, and interactions with Our Website.

2.2 How We Use Your Data

We use Your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain Our Service.
  • To process and manage Your newsletter subscriptions.
  • To analyze website traffic and improve Our Service using Google Analytics.
  • To process orders and payments (via Stripe).
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

2.3 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track and analyze user interactions with Our Website. This service collects:

  • IP address (may be anonymized), browser type, session duration, pages visited.
  • Device information (operating system, screen resolution, language settings).
  • Traffic sources (where visitors come from, referral data).
  • Geolocation data (approximate location based on IP address).
  • User behavior (clicks, time spent on pages, bounce rates, navigation paths).

What Data We Receive:

  • Aggregated reports on website traffic, user demographics, and engagement metrics.
  • Anonymized session data to analyze browsing trends.
  • Information on referral sources and conversion rates.

How to Opt-Out:
You can disable Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on or adjusting browser settings.

2.4 Newsletter and Marketing Emails

If You subscribe to Our newsletter, We may send You marketing emails. These emails may include tracking technologies such as web beacons, clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs that allow Us to:

  • Determine if an email was opened.
  • Track link clicks and engagement.
  • Analyze the popularity of content and improve Our communications.

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in the email or contacting Us directly.
